Hip-Hop Festival

BACK TO THE CITY AFRICA's BIGGEST HIP-HOP FESTIVAL

Attracting close to 20,000 festival goers each year, this is Africa's biggest hip hop and street culture festival.

Three live stages are spread across Mary Fitzgerald Square in downtown Joburg, with well-known MCs, beat boxers, rappers and dancers performing throughout the day.

There are also basketball courts, skating ramps, graffiti areas and a market selling cool T-shirts, caps and customised T-shirts.

If you are curious to find out what's cool among the new generation of 'Born Free' South Africans, this is the place.
