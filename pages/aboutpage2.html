About SoftRock Inc.

We are a full-service live sound rental, technical supply operation and production company, priding ourselves on providing excellent equipment, technical know how and friendly, professional service for all of our clients.
We have successfully supplied equipment and sound engineering services for artists such as Bastille, Seether, The Pixies, Of Monsters and Men, Johnny Clegg, Mango Groove, Freshly Ground and more.
